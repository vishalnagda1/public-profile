About

I'm a backend developer and project lead based in Udaipur, India, currently leading backend architecture and a team of developers building telehealth systems for TruDoc in Dubai. Over the last eight years I've architected the core of products in healthcare, sports media, AI and enterprise, owning everything from database design and microservices to SSO, caching and production deployments.

My comfort zone is the server side: designing APIs and gateways, splitting monoliths into services, wiring up authentication and integrations, and making sure things stay fast and secure under load. Lately that's meant a lot of Go, MySQL, Redis and Docker Swarm for healthcare infrastructure, and before that custom RAG pipelines and LLM tooling for enterprise AI.

I lead small, sharp teams, mentor developers, and turn fuzzy ideas into software that ships and quietly holds up long after launch. Along the way I picked up a law degree, which sharpened how I think about risk, systems and detail.

Currently focused on

  • Golang microservices for telehealth at scale
  • Hospital at Home (HAH) platform & SSO
  • WhatsApp health bots with n8n + Wati
  • Docker Swarm & Redis-backed infrastructure
